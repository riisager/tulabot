Impersonal.io

See true Google results, no matter where you are

When you perform a Google search, the results are almost always influenced by your history, location, and device. This makes it difficult for SEO specialists to see what customers *actually* see when they are in a specific city or country.

Impersonal.io solves this by letting you run completely depersonalized searches directly from specific cities or countries worldwide. This allows you to verify local rankings exactly as they appear to users on the street level.

Key features

  • Global reach: Perform searches as if you were in Copenhagen, New York, London, or Sydney.
  • Remove personalization: Get neutral results without the influence of past searches.
  • User-friendly interface: Enter your keyword and select your destination – and you're ready to go.
