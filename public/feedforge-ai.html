FeedForge AI

Automate, enrich, and optimize your product feed

A sharp, error-free, and information-rich product feed is the backbone of any successful Google Shopping (PMax) campaign. Poor titles, weak descriptions, and missing attributes cost you visibility and expensive clicks every single day.

FeedForge AI uses artificial intelligence to massage your heavy, unstructured product data into the perfect Google Merchant Center format. The tool transforms closed PIM exports into high-converting, searchable product stories.

What can FeedForge AI help with?

  • Title and description optimization: Automatically incorporates brand, product type, size, and colors directly into the title.
  • Error removal: Catches and fixes warnings that usually prevent products from being shown in Shopping at all.
  • Structuring raw data: Takes raw PIM data and structures it so Google's algorithms love it.
