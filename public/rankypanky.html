RankyPanky

AI Visibility Analytics Platform

For years, marketers have mastered SEO. But now customer behavior is changing—they aren't just searching Google; they are asking AI for direct recommendations. The AI's answer has become the new search result, directly influencing purchasing decisions.

Traditional SEO tools can't see inside this black box. RankyPanky was built to reverse-engineer LLMs (like ChatGPT and Gemini) to show you exactly where your brand ranks in AI conversations and who is beating you.

Turn AI's Black Box Into a Strategic Advantage

  • Define Your "AI Share of Voice": Get a concrete metric for your brand's authority and visibility inside language models.
  • Gap Analysis: Automatically identify topics and keywords where you are currently invisible to the AI, and discover the hidden competitors taking your place.
  • Strategy Validation: Discover which retailers the AI associates with you and see if your content efforts actually influence AI outputs over time.
