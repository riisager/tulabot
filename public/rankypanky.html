RankyPanky

Understand why rankings change with TF-IDF

In SEO, it's not enough to know *that* your rankings have dropped. You need to understand *why*. RankyPanky helps you dissect the actual content at the top of Google to find the patterns behind the ups and downs.

The tool uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency - Inverse Document Frequency) analysis to compare differences in SERP results over time or between different domains. It allows you to uncover which topics and terms your competitors are winning on.

Key benefits

  • Content focus: Provides mathematical insight into which words drive relevance in your specific niche.
  • Analyze fluctuations: Perfect for algorithm updates when the SERP changes drastically.
  • Competitor tracking: See exactly where competitors' text outperforms your own based on Google's current evaluation.
