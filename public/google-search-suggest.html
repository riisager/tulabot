Google Search Suggest Autocomplete

Smart keyword expansions directly from the source

When working with keyword research, there is no stronger signal than the suggestions Google itself gives users as they type. This Apify tool automates the collection of these Google Autocomplete suggestions.

The tool is built for SEO specialists and content creators who want to quickly map out all the long-tail variants, questions, and related searches that real people are currently using.

Key benefits

  • Directly from Google: The data comes from the world's largest search engine in real-time – not outdated third-party databases.
  • Deep expansion: Automatically appends letters, numbers, and question words to unearth the deepest and most specific suggestions.
  • Built to scale: Run thousands of seed keywords through Apify's infrastructure to rapidly build complete topic clusters.
