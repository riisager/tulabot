PIMplify

PIMP Your Product Data

Stop wasting time writing product descriptions manually. PIMplify uses generative AI to enrich your product data, helping you get products to market faster and with higher quality content.

Powered by Google Gemini

How It Works

  • Upload Your Excel File: Easily upload your product data. PIMplify works with standard Excel files exported from any PIM system.
  • AI-Powered Amplification: Select which fields to improve, provide optional guides, and let our AI generate compelling, high-quality product descriptions and attributes.
  • Export & Re-import: Review the AI-generated changes and export the amplified data back to an Excel file, ready to be re-imported into your PIM system.

Built for E-commerce Professionals

  • Increase Speed-to-Market: Automate the creation of product content and get your products online faster.
  • Improve Data Quality: Ensure consistent, high-quality, and SEO-friendly descriptions across your entire product catalog.
  • Control the Output: Use global and field-specific guides to steer the AI and match your brand's tone of voice.
  • Secure & Private: Your product data is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. Bring your own Gemini API key.
  • Factual, Accurate Content: Optionally enable Google Search grounding to fact-check and enrich your product data with real-time information from the web before generating new content.
