Redirect Mapping Tool

Automated Redirect Mapping with AI

Creating a redirect map during a major website launch, replatforming, or when deleting many pages can be one of the most time-consuming tasks for an SEO specialist. Mistakes here can cost you all organic traffic overnight.

This tool removes most of the manual work by using advanced TF-IDF analysis and AI to automatically match old URLs with the most relevant new URLs based on content relevance.

Key benefits

  • Huge time savings: Processes that previously took days in spreadsheets are now completed in minutes.
  • Focus on relevance: Machine learning ensures that link equity is passed to pages covering the absolute closest related topic.
  • Reduced human error: Minimize the risk of 404 errors and soft-404s after launch.
