Blssit.com

The Social Wishlist Platform

Blssit.com is designed to make gifting social, easy, and surprise-proof. Whether it's for birthdays, weddings, or just keeping track of things you love, Blssit provides the tools to manage your wishes beautifully.

Core Features

Wishlists & Wishes

Create Blsslists and add wishes (Blsses) with images, prices, links, priority, and specific variants like size or color.

Smart Sharing

Share lists with family and friends via a simple link. Control visibility with three levels: Private, Friends-only, or Public.

Social BlssFeed

Follow friends and stay updated on their wishes. See something you like? Add someone else's wish directly to your own list.

Gifting Without Duplicates

Reserve gifts so others know it's taken. Reservations are hidden from the list owner to keep the surprise intact.

Influencer Gifting

A specialized program allowing fans to send gifts via Stripe with automatic checkout and webhook handling.

Automatic Scraping

Paste any product link, and our system automatically fetches the title, price, and image for you.

Advanced Technical Capabilities

  • Image Cropping: Built-in tool to upload and perfect your item photos.
  • Multilingual Support: Fully localized in Danish and English using i18next.
  • Admin Oversight: Comprehensive panel for tracking purchases and applications.
  • Restoration: A trash bin that allows you to recover accidentally deleted wishes.
