Bulk HTTP Status Check

Check hundreds of URLs in seconds

When working with SEO or website migrations, it's critical to know exactly how your URLs respond. Do they return 200 OK, 404 Not Found, or do they redirect via 301/302?

This tool is designed for technical SEO specialists. You simply paste your list of URLs, and the tool checks them lightning-fast in bulk. No complex setups or desktop crawlers needed for a quick status check.

Why use this tool?

  • Speed: Checks large amounts of URLs in parallel.
  • Technical insight: Shows you precise HTTP status codes and the final destination URL (for redirects).
  • Directly in the browser: No need to download software like Screaming Frog for a quick status check.
